Coding in Nagaland

Coding is something new to our Naga society and is not that common. But I can't denied the fact that there will be someone out there who are pro-coders.

Coding sometime can be frustrating and sometime rewarding but we must have COMPANION to share our idea and skill to keep the journey alive.

Hence I'm trying to built a common platform do keep our coding alive and draw new coders.


Challenges For The Coders

The coding landscape in Nagaland can be challenging, burdened by technological limitations and, at times, a lack of understanding.

This can lead to a sense of isolation for those passionate about programming. However,we will try to address this very need. We're building a space where we tackle these issues head-on, sharing resources, troubleshooting together, and providing the necessary encouragement to flourish.

By uniting, we can reshape the perception of coding and establish a more supportive and technologically empowered environment for everyone.
